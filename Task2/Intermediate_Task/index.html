About SRH

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a franchise cricket team that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Based in Hyderabad, Telangana, the team was founded in 2012 and is known for its strong bowling attack and fighting spirit. SRH won its maiden IPL title in 2016 under the leadership of David Warner. Over the years, the team has featured star players like Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Kane Williamson, and Rashid Khan, gaining a loyal fanbase for their consistent performances and never-give-up attitude.
